Hi! My name is Jonathan Zachary and I am a fullstack web developer. I came to this carreer after two decades of studying and teaching chess. After I graduated from the coding bootcamp General Assembly I was asked to stay on as a teacher. It was a perfect fit with my past teaching experience and it allowed me to refine my understanding of web development by creating lessons and exercises that illustrate the core competencies of modern web development.

Studying and teaching chess for the past two decades has honed my ability solve abstract problems and communicate those solutions clearly. I have wide ranging interests from math to poetry. My ability to teach myself coupled with my curiosity has led me to a life-long love of learning. I enjoy building relationships based on exchanging knowledge, empathy, and communal problem solving
